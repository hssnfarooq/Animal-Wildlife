United States Of America

The list of indigenous species in the United States is extensive, and it contains well-known species such as coyotes and raccoons, as well as lesser-known characters such as the glass lizard and the cute pika. The United States is one of only 17 megadiverse nations in the world, with an estimated population of 2,972 species. The United States is home to over 400 recognized mammal species, nearly 800 bird species, over 300 reptile species, nearly 300 amphibian species, and over 1,100 fish species
