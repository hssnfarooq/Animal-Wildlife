India

India is a place of extraordinary extremes in terms of scale, population, and also wildlife. India's huge array of lush valleys, verdant grasslands, luscious tropical jungles and trees, thick lakes, and arid deserts is home to some of the world's greatest (and also smallest) biodiversity. The Himalayas, the Indo-Gangetic plain (named after the two northern rivers against which it is bound), the Western Ghats mountain chain, and the Deccan Plateau in the south are the country's most prominent areas.
