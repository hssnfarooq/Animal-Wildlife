United Arab Emirates

The UAE is home to a diverse range of habitats and animals, from mountains and mangroves to desert and coastline. Many animals survive in the UAE's harsh environments, developing interesting adaptations to deal with the heat and landscape. However, the rapid rate of unplanned growth and expanded human-wildlife activities is resulting in the disappearance of important ecosystems and the expansion of the list of species of special concern.
